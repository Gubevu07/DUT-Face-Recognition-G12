How Our System Works

1. Register Profile

Students complete a one-time registration with their details and a secure face scan.

2. Scan Face

Students simply scan their face on the camera to get marked present instantly.

3. Attendance Marked

The system verifies the identity and marks the student 'present' in real-time, securely and accurately.

4. Lecturer Dashboard

Lecturers can log in to view attendance dashboards, track student performance, and allocate marks.
