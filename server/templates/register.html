Terms and Conditions for Biometric Data Use

  1. Purpose: Your facial data will be used solely for recording attendance for your registered modules at DUT.
  2. Storage: Your data will be converted into a secure numerical vector and stored in an encrypted database.
  3. Access: Access is restricted to authorized administrators and lecturers for attendance purposes only.
  4. Consent: By checking the box, you provide explicit consent for the use of your biometric data as described.
